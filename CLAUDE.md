## Design Context (via Impeccable)

### Users
- **Primary Persona:** Competitive, social individuals (18-35) in Cairo/Heliopolis.
- **Context of Use:** Primarily mobile. Users are often "on the ground" or near the field, needing to quickly check availability and lock a slot.
- **Job to be Done:** Convert curiosity into a secured mission slot with zero friction.

### Brand Personality
- **Voice:** Energetic, direct, and competitive.
- **3-Word Personality:** **PLAY / ON GROUND / WIN**.
- **Emotional Goal:** **Playful Arcade Adrenaline**. The interface should feel like picking a character in a high-def arcade game—fun, social, and neon-charged.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual Tone:** "High-Octane Tactical Arcade". A fusion of gritty military interfaces and vibrant esports neon.
- **Visual Hierarchy:** Deep blacks populated by luminous green/orange accents.
- **Motion Strategy:** Balanced. High-impact "Catchy" motions for the Hero and major section transitions, shifting to "Tactical Precision" (fast, efficient, UI-only motion) for the Booking Wizard to ensure performance and speed.

### Design Principles
1. **Performance over Slop:** Every animation must earn its place. Restrain motion in data-heavy phases (Booking) to ensure mobile speed.
2. **Neon Functionalism:** Colors (Neon Green/Orange) are not just decorative; they signal hierarchy, engagement, and success.
3. **Tactical Authenticity:** Avoid generic "enterprise" UI patterns. Use clipped corners, monospace accents, and HUD-inspired frames.
4. **Touch-First Engagement:** Since use is primarily mobile "on the ground," buttons and inputs must be sized for high-adrenaline fingertip interaction.
