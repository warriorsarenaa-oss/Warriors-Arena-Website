# FAQ — Warriors Arena (English)

> Eight questions covering the most common customer concerns. Load into `messages/en.json` under `faq.items`.

## Q1. What is Warriors Arena?

Warriors Arena is an indoor/outdoor action venue in Heliopolis, Cairo, offering two experiences: Laser Tag and Gel Blasters. Both are designed for groups of friends, birthday parties, corporate events, and family outings. We are located inside Maryland Park.

## Q2. How old do players need to be?

Laser Tag is suitable for ages 7 and up. Gel Blasters is recommended for ages 12 and up because the blasters fire harder. Groups mixing younger and older players should choose Laser Tag. Adult supervision is welcome for all sessions.

## Q3. How much does a session cost?

Laser Tag starts at 150 EGP per player for 30 minutes, or 300 EGP per player for 60 minutes. Gel Blasters is 100 EGP per player for 30 minutes. Groups of up to 6 players can book together. Special bundle offers appear on our homepage when available.

## Q4. Do I need to pay the full amount upfront?

No. You only pay a 25% deposit to confirm your reservation. The remaining balance is paid at the venue on the day of your game. The deposit is sent via InstaPay after you complete your online booking — instructions and contact details are shown on your confirmation screen and emailed receipt.

## Q5. What happens if I need to cancel or reschedule?

You can cancel or reschedule by contacting us on WhatsApp up to 6 hours before your session. Cancellations more than 6 hours in advance receive a full deposit refund. Cancellations within 6 hours or no-shows forfeit the deposit. If we have to cancel (due to weather or equipment issues), you get a full refund and our apologies.

## Q6. Is there a park entrance fee?

Yes. Maryland Park charges 30 EGP per person on regular days and 50 EGP per person on public holidays and festival days. This fee is paid directly at the park gate and is separate from your Warriors Arena booking.

## Q7. What should I wear?

Comfortable closed-toe shoes (sneakers are best), clothes you can move freely in, and layers you don't mind getting dusty. For Gel Blasters, long sleeves and long pants are recommended — the gel balls are soft and water-based but can sting on bare skin at close range. All safety gear is provided.

## Q8. Can I book for a birthday party or corporate event?

Absolutely. Bundles for 4–6 players are the easiest starting point. For larger groups, private-venue buyouts, or custom formats, send us a WhatsApp message and we'll build a quote. Birthday decorations can be arranged with notice. We don't currently offer in-house catering, but you're welcome to bring your own food.
