## 1. Who We Are

Warriors Arena ("we", "us", "our") operates a recreational venue located inside Maryland Park, Heliopolis, Cairo, Egypt. We provide laser tag and gel blaster game experiences that customers can book through our website (the "Service").

By using our Service or visiting our venue, you agree to these Terms and our Privacy Policy below.

## 2. Bookings

### 2.1 Making a Reservation

You can book through our website by selecting a game type, time slot, and number of players, then providing your contact information. After you submit, we send you a booking code and instructions to send a 25% deposit.

### 2.2 Deposit

A 25% deposit is required to confirm your reservation. Deposits are sent via InstaPay to the handle shown on your booking confirmation. The remaining balance is paid at the venue on the day of your game.

Reservations are created as "confirmed" immediately. If we do not receive your deposit, we reserve the right to cancel your reservation and release your slot.

### 2.3 Accuracy of Information

You are responsible for providing accurate contact information (name, phone number, email). We use this information to contact you about your booking. Inaccurate information may result in your booking being cancelled.

### 2.4 Age Restrictions

Laser Tag is suitable for players aged 7 and up. Gel Blasters is recommended for players aged 12 and up. Players under 16 should have a parent or guardian aware of the booking. Warriors Arena reserves the right to refuse entry to anyone who we believe is too young to safely participate.

## 3. Cancellation and Refund Policy

### 3.1 Customer Cancellations

- **More than 6 hours before your slot:** Cancel or reschedule by contacting us on WhatsApp. Your deposit will be refunded in full.
- **Less than 6 hours before your slot:** The deposit is forfeited and will not be refunded.
- **No-shows:** If you do not arrive at your booked time, the deposit is forfeited.

### 3.2 Cancellations By Us

If we cancel your booking due to weather, equipment failure, or any other reason on our side, we will refund your deposit in full and work with you to reschedule if you wish.

### 3.3 Rescheduling

Rescheduling is treated the same as cancellation in terms of timing rules. Reschedule more than 6 hours before your slot and we will move your deposit to your new booking. Within 6 hours, rescheduling is at our discretion.

## 4. Venue Rules and Safety

### 4.1 Safety Briefing

All players receive a safety briefing before every game. Participation in the briefing is mandatory.

### 4.2 Provided Equipment

Warriors Arena provides all game equipment (blasters, vests, eye protection). You must use the equipment as instructed and return it in good condition after your session. Deliberate damage to equipment may result in charges for repair or replacement.

### 4.3 Clothing

We recommend closed-toe shoes and comfortable athletic clothing. For Gel Blasters, long sleeves and long pants are strongly recommended. Open-toe shoes (sandals, flip-flops) are not permitted in the arena for safety reasons.

### 4.4 Health Restrictions

Please do not participate if you have a recent injury, heart condition, respiratory condition, or are pregnant, without consulting a medical professional first. Warriors Arena is not responsible for medical issues arising from participation.

### 4.5 Behavior

We reserve the right to remove any player who behaves in a way that endangers themselves or others, damages equipment, or disrupts other customers. No refunds are given in these cases.

### 4.6 Alcohol and Substances

Entry is refused to anyone under the influence of alcohol or other substances. No refunds.

## 5. Park Entrance Fee

Warriors Arena operates inside Maryland Park. The park charges a separate entrance fee (30 EGP per person on regular days, 50 EGP on holidays), which is not included in your booking and is paid at the park gate directly to the park operator.
